Our Story

Dressing the
Community

House of Style has been a cornerstone of fashion in West New York, New Jersey — serving the community with carefully curated men's and women's clothing. We believe that dressing well is a form of self-expression, and we're here to help you find yours.

With two dedicated boutiques on Bergenline Avenue — one for men, one for women — we offer an intimate, personalized shopping experience with collections that span from everyday essentials to statement pieces for every occasion.
